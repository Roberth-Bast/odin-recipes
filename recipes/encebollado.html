Encebollado

Description

A classic from the Ecuadorian coast. Prepare it to share with your whole family. Enjoy this delicious dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 Tablespoon Vegetable Oil
  • 1 1/2 Cups Red Onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 ripe kidney tomatoes, quartered
  • 1/2 Tablespoon Ground Cumin
  • 2 Teaspoons Dry Chili
  • 1 1/2 Liter Water
  • 4 tablespoons finely chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 pound fresh albacore, loins
  • 2 Cups of diced yuca
  • 2/3 Cup Red Bell Pepper, coarsely chopped
  • 2/3 Cup Green Bell Pepper, Coarsely Chopped
  • 1/2 Cup Celery, roughly chopped
  • 2 Maggi速 Vegetable Broth Cubes
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 4 Units Lemon cut into quarters

Steps

  1. Place the water and oil in a pot to boil and add 3 chopped tomatoes and half a paite単a onion,
    add the celery, the pieces of pepper, 2 tablespoons of cilantro, garlic, cumin, salt, pepper and chili to dry.

  2. In another separate pot, cook the previously chopped cassava with plenty of water until soft.

  3. Separately, make the onion pickle with the remaining finely chopped paite単a onion, washed, drained, and seasoned with salt, lemon juice,
    and the remaining chopped cilantro. Let it sit until it's cured.

  4. Add the albacore loins to the broth from step 1, cook them completely,
    and remove from the pot once they are well cooked, approximately 10 minutes.

  5. After setting aside the cooked albacore, remove the vegetables from the broth and place them in a blender. Add a little cooked yuca and a little of the yuca cooking liquid,
    blending until smooth. Strain the blended liquid over the broth in the pot and add the yuca cooking water.
    Dissolve the MAGGI速 Vegetable Broth cubes. Bring to a boil for 5 minutes over medium heat. Return the albacore to the preparation.

  6. Season with salt and pepper. Place a small amount of soup with chunks of yuca and albacore
    on the plate and serve with some of the onion pickle you prepared earlier.
