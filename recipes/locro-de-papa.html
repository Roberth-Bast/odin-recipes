Locro de papa

Description

From the mountains of Ecuador comes a traditional dish that's delicious and easy to prepare. Don't know how to make it? Don't worry!
Here, we'll share the secrets that will make potatoes, cheese, and all the Ecuadorian flavors the stars of your dish.

  • 2 Teaspoons Annatto Oil
  • 2 Teaspoons finely chopped pearl onion
  • 2 1/2 Cups Peeled and Diced Potatoes

    Once they have browned, add the water.
