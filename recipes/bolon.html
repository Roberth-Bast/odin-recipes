Bolon Recipe

Description

El Bolón de verde es una delicia ecuatoriana, bolitas hechas con plátano verde y rellenas de queso.
Es una gran opción para acompañar el desayuno junto con huevos y café.
El Queso Ecuatoriano Tropical es ideal para esta receta por su textura y sabor auténticos.

Ingredients

  • 4 green bananas
  • 4 butter tablespoon
  • 4 oz. tropical ecuadorian cheese collapsed
  • Salt to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Steps

  1. Peel and cut the plantains into 1 1/2" thick pieces.
    Heat the oil in a large skillet and fry the plantains until golden brown and tender.

  2. Transfer the plantains to a bowl and add the butter, salt, and pepper.
    Mash them until smooth.

  3. Using your hands, form small balls with the plantain dough. With your fingers, make a hole in the center of each ball and fill it with Tropical Ecuadorian Cheese.
    Then close it to form the ball again.

  4. Heat more vegetable oil and fry the balls, turning them carefully
    until golden brown and crispy. Serve hot.
